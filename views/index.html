About

Chatterbox is a free chat framework created by Chatterbox. The download includes the complete source files including the nodejs project and the sample template for easy customization.

Whether you're a student looking to add a chat feature to your application, a professional looking to introduce quality chat options, or a curious soul looking to learn nodeJs, this project is the perfect starting point!
